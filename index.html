About Us

Xplicit Air is a specialized HVAC solutions provider focused exclusively on industrial environments. Based in Durban, South Africa, we design, install, and maintain high-performance systems for factories, warehouses, and production plants — ensuring energy efficiency and operational reliability.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians leverage cutting-edge technology and industry best practices to deliver customized solutions that meet the unique demands of large-scale industrial operations. From initial design to ongoing maintenance, Xplicit Air is your trusted partner for all industrial HVAC needs.
